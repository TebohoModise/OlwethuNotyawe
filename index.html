Olwethu Notyawe

Olwethu is an aspiring Educator from Mtata in the eastern cape, she spent most of her childhood in Sasolborg, a small town in the free state where she discovered her passion for Teaching, she is currently doing her Bachelor in education degree at the Nelson Mandela University in Gqeberha, where she also plans to reside upon completion of her degree.
